Nexgen BPO

An AI voice agent is made up of four core components that work together to carry out a full voice interaction from start to finish.

Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR)

Turns voice into text, removes noise, supports 50+ languages.

Language Understanding (LLM + NLU)

Finds intent, extracts names, dates, account info.

Dialogue Management and Logic

Decides next action: answer, book, escalate, sync with CRM.

Neural Text-to-Speech (Neural TTS)

Speaks naturally, with emotion and pause control.
